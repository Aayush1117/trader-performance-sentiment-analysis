# 📊 Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

## 🔹 Objective

Analyze how market sentiment (Fear vs Greed) impacts trader behavior and performance.

---

## 🔹 Dataset

* Historical trading data
* Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

---

## 🔹 Methodology

* Cleaned and merged datasets
* Created key metrics: PnL, win rate, trade size, trade frequency
* Performed sentiment-based analysis
* Segmented traders (frequent/infrequent, consistent/inconsistent, trade size)
* Built a Random Forest model to predict trade profitability

---

## 🔹 Key Insights

* Traders perform better during Greed periods
* Trading activity increases during Greed
* Fear periods show higher volatility and risk
* Larger trades are associated with higher PnL variability

---

## 🔹 Strategy Recommendations

* Reduce trade size during Fear markets
* Increase trading activity during Greed conditions
* Manage large trades carefully due to higher risk

---

## 🔹 Model (Bonus)

Random Forest classifier used to predict trade profitability.

---

## 🔹 Summary

### Methodology

* Cleaned and merged trading and sentiment datasets using date alignment
* Created features like trade size, win rate, and sentiment score
* Performed sentiment-based and behavioral analysis
* Built a Random Forest model for trade outcome prediction

### Insights

* Traders perform better during Greed periods
* Trading activity increases during positive sentiment
* Fear periods show higher volatility and inconsistent outcomes
* Larger trades are associated with higher risk

### Strategy Recommendations

* Reduce trade size during Fear markets to manage risk
* Increase trading activity during Greed conditions
* Avoid large position sizes unless risk tolerance is high
